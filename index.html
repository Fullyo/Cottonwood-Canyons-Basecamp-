Explore Little Cottonwood Canyon

World-famous skiing, epic mountain biking, and stunning hikes are right outside your door.

Legendary Skiing

Ski or ride "The Greatest Snow on Earth®" at Alta and Snowbird, both just minutes away from the basecamp.

Mountain Biking

In the summer, the canyon transforms into a biker's paradise with miles of singletrack and lift-accessed trails.

Alpine Hiking

Explore stunning trails to alpine lakes like Cecret Lake, surrounded by wildflower-filled meadows in the summer.

Proximity to SLC

Enjoy the best of both worlds with Salt Lake City's vibrant dining and culture just a 30-minute drive away.
